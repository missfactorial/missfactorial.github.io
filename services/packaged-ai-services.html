Business Performance Dashboards

If you can't measure it, you can't improve it.

We design custom business dashboards that turn raw data into clear, actionable insights. From stakeholder interviews to dashboard deployment, we ensure that executives, managers, and teams can monitor the metrics and KPIs that matter most in real time.

What's Included – From Strategy to Delivery
  • Discovery Call: Understand KPIs and decision-making workflows
  • Data Audit: Evaluate source quality, accessibility, and integration complexity
  • Design Mockups: Create 1–2 dashboard prototypes (2 rounds of revision)
  • Dashboard Development: Platform of your choice (e.g., Looker Studio, Tableau, Streamlit, etc.)
  • Final Revisions: Incorporate your feedback
  • Training & Adoption: Onboarding session for your team
  • Documentation: Admin guide and best practices
  • Post-Launch Support: 30 days of support for any tweaks or questions
Popular Use Cases By Industry
  • Retail & eCommerce: Understand top-performing products, reduce stockouts, and spot trends
  • SaaS & Tech: Monitor churn, feature adoption, and recurring revenue in real time
  • Healthcare: Track patient flow, scheduling efficiency, and resource allocation
  • Nonprofits: Measure campaign impact, donor engagement, and funding progress
  • Logistics & Operations: Identify delivery delays, fleet inefficiencies, and supply chain bottlenecks
  • Marketing Teams: Analyze ROI by channel, conversion rates, and lead funnel performance

Segmentation & Behavioral Insights

Discover segments, trends, and untapped opportunities.

Whether you're launching a new campaign, refining your product strategy, or just trying to understand your audience better, segmentation gives you clarity where you once had noise.
