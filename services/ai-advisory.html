Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our AI advisory services

How do I prepare for my advisory session?

To make the most of your session, we recommend preparing a list of specific questions or challenges you'd like to address. For career sessions, have your resume and career goals ready. For business sessions, gather information about your current processes and specific areas where you think AI could help.

Do I need technical knowledge to benefit from these sessions?

Not at all! Our sessions are designed for people at all technical levels. Whether you're a technical expert or completely new to AI, we'll meet you where you are and provide guidance that matches your understanding and needs.

What happens after the advisory session?

After your session, you'll receive a summary of the discussion and recommendations, including your personalized roadmap. We also offer follow-up sessions for those who want to track progress or address new challenges that arise during implementation.
