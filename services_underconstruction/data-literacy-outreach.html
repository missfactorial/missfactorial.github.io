Data Literacy Outreach

Empower your community with essential data literacy skills through our engaging workshops, presentations, and educational initiatives designed to make AI and data science accessible to everyone.

Bridging the Data Literacy Gap

In today's data-driven world, understanding how to interpret and use data is becoming as fundamental as reading and writing. Our Data Literacy Outreach programs are designed to democratize access to these essential skills, ensuring that communities and organizations of all types can harness the power of data for positive impact.

Our Outreach Programs

We offer a variety of outreach formats to meet the needs of different communities and organizations:

Community Workshops

Interactive, hands-on sessions that introduce data literacy concepts to community members in an accessible, engaging way. These workshops can be customized for various age groups and knowledge levels.

Speaker Series

Thought-provoking presentations on data literacy topics, designed to inspire and educate audiences about the importance of data in everyday life and decision-making.

Educational Programs

Structured learning experiences for schools, libraries, and community centers that build data literacy skills through age-appropriate activities and curriculum.

Train-the-Trainer

Programs that equip community leaders, educators, and organizational staff with the knowledge and resources to teach data literacy within their own communities.

Topics We Cover

Our outreach programs can address a wide range of data literacy topics, including:

  • Data Basics: Understanding what data is, where it comes from, and how it's used
  • Critical Data Consumption: How to evaluate data-based claims and identify misinformation
  • Data Visualization: Reading and interpreting charts, graphs, and other visual representations of data
  • AI Awareness: Understanding what AI is, how it works, and its impact on society
  • Data Privacy: Protecting personal information and understanding data rights
  • Data for Decision Making: Using data to make informed choices in personal and professional contexts
  • Data Ethics: Exploring the ethical implications of data collection and use

Our Approach

All of our outreach programs are built on these core principles:

  • Accessibility: We make complex concepts understandable for audiences of all backgrounds.
  • Engagement: We use interactive, hands-on methods to keep participants actively involved.
  • Relevance: We connect data literacy to real-world applications that matter to participants.
  • Empowerment: We focus on building confidence and agency in working with data.
  • Inclusivity: We design our programs to be welcoming and accessible to diverse audiences.
