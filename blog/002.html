AI and Beer: A Perfect Pairing at A Seis Manos

  • Date: April 15, 2023
  • Location: Bogotá, Colombia

    • I had the pleasure of hosting a talk combining two exciting topics—beer and technology! During this session, I explored how artificial intelligence and algorithms can help people find the perfect beer. We delved into the fascinating world of vector representations of abstract concepts—which enable algorithms to identify patterns and similarities, whether between beers or words in different languages.
    We had a full house. It was a fun, family-friendly event, free and open to all. The lively audience learned how technology can enhance even the simplest pleasures in life, like enjoying a cold beer. Looking forward to more events like this!
