Meet the Founder

Viviana R. Márquez

EdTech Founder | Data Scientist | Lecturer

Meet Viviana, a distinguished data scientist, lecturer, and the dynamic force behind Miss Factorial Academy. With a Master's degree in Data Science from the University of San Francisco, a decade of coding experience, and a rich background in mathematics, Viviana has carved a niche in both academic and industry circles. Her professional journey spans significant roles in advertising, marketing, retail, and cybersecurity with industry leaders like Dataminr and HBO Latin America, where she developed cutting-edge AI and machine learning solutions. Beyond her industry impact, Viviana’s flair for communication, honed through her experience as a TV and Radio host in various countries, enables her to deliver data science concepts with unparalleled clarity and engagement. Having taught over 1,300 students worldwide across diverse educational levels, her innovative teaching methods and commitment to making data fluency accessible and genuinely fun set her apart.


Languages: English, Spanish, Portuguese, and intermediate French.
